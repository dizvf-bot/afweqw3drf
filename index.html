About the Bitlife game

Welcome to Bitlife which is a life simulator and is also well-known with the name of Bitlife: Life Simulator. In the game, you can live for yourself and decide any choice from family to work. Simulate your life in this digital Bitlife world!

Are you ready to live a second life? Are there too many distractions in life right now? Do you get tired of living with so many responsibilities on your shoulders? Your life may not be what you want it to be. Come to this game to experience a new life. Every choice in this new life is left to you to choose without being derived by any other factors. You can also become a citizen or whoever you want. The decision is up to you. Play Bitlife: Life Simulator game now online for free. No download, installation, or registration is required.

Essential Bitlife's information for you in the main menu

Your main menu consists of 3 sections: New Life, Stats, and Configure. Go to each section to track and get the necessary information for the game.

  • First, you can start a new life which is the main goal of every player when coming to this game. You can create a new life easily. You only need one tap to start a new life.
  • Second, in the Stats section, you can monitor ribbons. You can earn 30 different ribbons in this simulator game. These 30 ribbons are always ready and just waiting for you to succeed. There are some notable ribbons like Rich, Hero, Famous, or Successful. These are all traits that many people wish they had.
  • Besides, there are many other great ribbons like Globetrotter, etc. Can you gain all 30 of these ribbons?
  • Third, in the Configure section, you can customize people to make your friends appear in the game or set sound effects for this game.

Start a new life from birth

After you decide to start a new life, the Bitlife: Life Simulator game gives you a profile of a newborn. You will start from scratch. You will know basic information like name, gender, age, and location. Besides, other information such as pets, or birthdays will also be provided. The game will decide on this basic information.

Besides, you should pay attention to some of your indicators when starting a new life. There are 4 stats including happiness, health, smarts, and looks. Each stat is randomly evaluated when you begin. They represent four different aspects of your life: feelings, health, intelligence, and physical appearance. These are probably also important criteria about a person. These indicators are as high as 100%. The higher the index is, the happier and richer your life will be. On the contrary, your life is poor if these indexes are low. Health stat is the most important. If this stat is 0%, it's over.

Continue your life with different events

Bitlife game allows you to live the way you want by tapping the Age button in the middle of the screen. After each press, you will add one year old. Of course, there will be different events happening in each of these years. There may be some problems. Moving house or your parents changing jobs can happen. Each of these events will impact your life. However, how you adapt to these is up to you.

Activities

You will have different activities to do. However, you cannot do it all at once. There are some activities that are only open when you reach a certain milestone. There are some activities that can only be activated when you meet the accompanying conditions such as health or finance. There are many different activities. After you decide to operate, various options will show up. You should choose the appropriate action because every action you take will affect your life. They will determine what kind of person you become. If you go to see a doctor, you can get health results or get treatment for diseases. Therefore, you should decide your future direction before doing something.

Assets and Relationships in the game

In addition to adding age, you can access the assets and relationships section. In this game, you can buy two main assets in the Assets section. Earn enough money and visit this section to buy what you want. In the Relationships section, you can track information about people related to you such as your father, mother, siblings, and pets. You can view each person's profile. Your relationships can increase over time. In addition, you can choose what activities you want to do with the people in your relationships.

Some notes when playing the online Bitlife game

Tips for playing

  • You should pay special attention to your stats mentioned above: Happiness, Health, Smarts, and Looks. If these indicators are good, it will be green. These indicators gradually decrease and hit alarming levels when they turn red.
  • These stats only increase or decrease as you perform different actions. If you do not do anything, they can change too. The reason is that there are unexpected events happening. If you don't do anything, these problems may affect your in-game status.
  • In this game, you should pay attention to your Health status and take good care of it.
